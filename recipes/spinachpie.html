Sunny Spinach Pie Recipe

Sunny Spinach Pie

This spinach pie is a family favourite! Its savoury scent is bound to make your mouth water and your tastebuds jump for joy. My mom always made this on a cold winter day to warm us up and I hope your family will appreciate one of her favourites!

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 3 ½ cup/500 g of flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 cup of warm dry white wine
  • 2 tsp of salt

For the Stuffing

  • 4 cups of sautee spinach with sauteed shallots/onions
  • 350 gr of ricotta cheese.
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 100 g of grated Parmigiano Reggiano or Grana cheese
  • a pinch of salt, pepper and spices
  • breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients for the dough and work the dough until it becomes uniform
  2. Cover it with plastic wrap while preparing the stuffing.
  3. Boil the spinach and squeeze it to eliminate the water.
  4. Mix it with the ricotta cheese.
  5. Add egg yolks
  6. Add the grated cheese, salt and pepper.
  7. Amalgamate
  8. Preheat the oven to 180°C/356°F.
  9. Divide the dough in two.
  10. Create 2 circles of 30cm/11.8in each using a rolling pin.
  11. Place the 1st dough circle on baking paper.
  12. Spread some breadcrumbs on it.
  13. Place part of the stuffing in the center of the circle and the remaining creating another circle around
  14. Cover it with the 2nd circle of dough.
  15. Wet the borders and attach one to another using a fork.
  16. Place a small bowl over the central stuffing and punch with a fork all around.
  17. Using a sharp knife cut the borders in 3cm/1.2in slices.
  18. Turn them upside 90°.
  19. Bake for 30 minutes in preheated oven at 180°C/356°F.
  20. Buon appetito!
